Chapter 01 — The shift

The CJM is not a poster anymore

Old way: a static map drawn once a year. New way: a living system that learns and updates itself in real time.

❌ Old CJM (yesterday)

  • Made once, stays the same
  • Same path for every customer
  • Built from guesses + interviews
  • Updated once a year, maybe
  • Lives in a Miro board nobody opens

✅ New CJM (with AI)

  • Updates itself every second
  • Personal path for each person
  • Built from real behavior data
  • Learns from every click
  • Drives real decisions live

Stage 01 — 👀 Awareness

AI finds your future customer

Old: you set ad targeting by hand — age, city, interests. Pray it works.

New: AI tests 10,000 ad variations in 24 hours. It finds the exact face, headline and emotion that makes your buyer click.

What AI does in this stage:

Generates ad copy + images, picks the best audience, allocates budget per platform — in real time.

The bot becomes a friend

Today's bots follow a script. Tomorrow's bot talks like a real human, remembers what you said yesterday, and answers questions about your specific goals.

What AI does in this stage:

LLM-powered chat, real-time Q&A, personalized recommendations ("based on what you said, try the Safety Manager track").

Stage 03 — 💪 Decision

AI predicts who will buy

AI looks at how you behaved — how fast you watched, where you paused, what tasks you got right.

It scores you: "87% chance to buy in 3 days". Sales focuses on hot leads. Bot sends a different path to cold ones.

What AI does in this stage:

Lead scoring, churn prediction, decides best time + best message + best channel for each person.

Predictive scoringBehavioral analyticsSend-time optimization
Stage 04 — 💳 Action

Every offer is different

Old: everyone gets the same 15% discount. New: AI decides the perfect offer for each person.

One user gets 15% off. Another hesitant user gets 20% + a 3-month payment plan. A price-sensitive student gets a scholarship application.

What AI does in this stage:

Dynamic pricing, personalized bundles, smart upsells, real-time A/B testing of checkout copy.

Dynamic pricingPersonalized bundlesSmart upsell timing
Stage 05 — ❤️ Loyalty

AI keeps customers from leaving

AI watches every action after the purchase. Did you log in? Did you finish Module 2? Did you stop replying?

If risk goes up, AI sends a personal message before you cancel. It also spots future ambassadors and gives them VIP treatment.

What AI does in this stage:

Churn prediction, auto-retention messages, find future ambassadors, personalized referral incentives.

Chapter 04 — Be careful

AI is not magic — risks & limits

🎭

No empathy = fake

If AI sounds robotic, customers leave faster. The human touch still wins.

🔒

Privacy & trust

Tracking every click is powerful — and scary. Be transparent or lose trust forever.

⚙️

Over-automation

If everything is automated, no one notices when something breaks. Keep humans in the loop.
